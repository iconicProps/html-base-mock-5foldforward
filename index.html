Be a part of something much bigger than a building – Discover your Function within His Body

At Five Fold Forward, we believe that the only way forward for the world-wide church of Jesus Christ – is to re-discover and bring a fresh understanding to the original blueprint that Jesus Himself gave to His Church – the five fold ministry as described in Eph 4:11-16. This blueprint, this model of ministry, is how the early church turned the world upside down and transformed culture everywhere they went. Where religion and traditions of man have lost the culture war in our current day, we believe that implementing this scriptural mandate can and will turn the tide back in Heavens favor and bring lasting transformation to the world around us.

Scriptural Foundation

  • Key Verses: The concept of the five fold ministry is found in Ephesians 4:11-16. Other important verses describing the relationship between the five fold ministry and spiritual gifts are Ephesians 2:20-22, 1 Corinthians 12:4-11, 27-31, Romans 12:6-8.
  • The text attributes these roles as gifts from Christ to the church.
  • The Apostle Paul wrote about these roles to the church in Ephesus, explaining their purpose.
  • The five roles:
    • Apostles
    • Prophets
    • Evangelists
    • Pastors
    • Teachers
  • Purpose: To equip and train all believers in spiritual gifts and disciplines for the work of service, to build up the body of Christ, and to bring every believer into full maturity and unity of the faith.
